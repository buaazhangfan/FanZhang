About Me

I got the Master of Engineering degree in Electrical Engineering and Computer Science (EECS) from University of California, Berkeley in 2019 under the supervision of Dr. Allen Y. Yang in the FHL Vive Center for Enhanced Reality. I focused on Visual Computing including Virtual Reality(VR), Human-Robot Interaction(HRI) and Computer Graphics(CG). Prior to that, I got the Bachelor of Engineering degree from Beihang University, China in 2018 majoring in Instrumentation and Optoelectronic Engineering, where I was fortunate to work with Prof. Shiping Zhu in Image Processing and Computer Vision. I exchanged at Polytechnic University of Milan (Politecnico di Milano), Italy in 2017 and worked with Prof. Emanuele Zappa on Vision-based 3D Measurement. I am interested in Computer Vision, Computer Graphics, Human-Computer Interaction and Human-Robot Interaction.
